Day 1: Journey Begins

Title description, November 17, 2021

This will be the entry for my first day of journaling on my medical journey of feeling better. This morning I woke up not feeling to hot a bit a brain fog, and some anxiety. I went to bed the night previous at about 1ish and slept till 10 pretty much uninterrupted. Same old nightmares that I usually have. I had nothing for breakfest. For lunch I had 2 bison patties and 2 cups of Spanish Rice. Overall my stomach felt fign with the meat, but when I started eating the rice it went south quickly. My vision was pretty poor today, with floaters, and a ton of blurriness. For dinner I had ground beef, and white rice with salt and hot sauce. Felt great after that, currently journaling before heading into bed, feeling pretty good. Had therapy today, really drains me and causes a ton of brain fog that typically goes away after some laying down. Tomorrow will be my first day back in the gym after just walking a little.
